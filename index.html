From Vision to Reality

North Carolina has created statutory flexibility for HR transformation. OSHR is developing policies and building infrastructure. But policy and infrastructure alone do not guarantee agencies will successfully recruit, develop, and retain the talent needed to serve citizens.

The delivery unit bridges the gap between policy intent and lived reality. It brings discipline, rigor, and user-centered design to the messy, complex work of organizational change. It learns by doing, scales what works, and fails fast when solutions miss the mark.

This is how North Carolina moves from having a vision for HR transformation to actually transforming HR‚Äîone intervention at a time.
